About

I am a Lecturer (Assistant Professor) in Financial Economics at the Adam Smith Business School and an Advisor at the Alan Turing Institute. Before joining Glasgow, I worked as a Quant for an asset management firm in London, and have taught at the School of Economics of the University of Nottingham. I was a post-doc researcher at the University of Freiburg after completing a Ph.D. in Financial Economics at the University of Glasgow. My teaching & research interests lie in the intersection of macroeconomics, finance, and applied econometrics.
