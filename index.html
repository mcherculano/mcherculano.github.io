About me

I am currently finishing my PhD studies in Financial Economics at the Department of Economics of the University of Glasgow in the United Kingdom, working on my thesis on the subjects of Systemic Risk and Macroeconomics. My research interests involve the study of topics in Empirical Macroeconomics and Finance, bringing economic theory to data by using the most recent advances in (Bayesian) econometrics.
