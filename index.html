About me

I am finishing my PhD in Financial Economics at the Department of Economics of the University of Glasgow and am affiliated with the University of Freiburg. My research interests involve the study of topics in Empirical Macroeconomics and Finance, bringing economic theory to data by using the most recent advances in (Bayesian) econometrics.
